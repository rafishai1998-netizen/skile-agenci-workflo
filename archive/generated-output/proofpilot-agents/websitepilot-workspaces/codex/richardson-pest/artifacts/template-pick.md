# Template Pick

## Selected style family

`heroic-branded-conversion`

Richardson Pest Management has the exact ingredients this family was built for: urgent local service, a strong black-and-amber emblem, a memorable scorpion safety story, same-day availability, and a high-conviction phone CTA. The current site already has the right proof, but the proof is hidden inside a soft Weebly layout. The improved system should make the brand feel protective, fast, local, and specific.

## Selected scaffold

`rockin-family-home-service` from `websitepilot/templates/sources/austinrockinshauling`

The selector also scored the rugged industrial scaffold highly, but this client should not feel like demolition or heavy equipment. The better translation is a family-owned home-service conversion page: strong hero, owner story, service grid, clear process, repeated CTA, and local trust. The scaffold is used for structure and conversion rhythm only. The final implementation must replace the source look with Richardson's own brand palette, real photos, scorpion motif, and verified copy.

## Why not copy source aesthetics directly

The goal is not to make Richardson look like Rockin S, 180 Sites, Anomaly, or any portfolio source. The reusable lesson is the code-level design pattern:

- Hero with strong local promise and immediate call action.
- Brand-colored motif that makes the page recognizable without needing the logo.
- Service cards that feel built, not dropped in.
- Story and proof sections that explain why this operator exists.
- Mobile-first CTA access.

## Remove-the-logo test target

If the logo is removed, the page should still read as Richardson Pest Management because of the black-and-amber scorpion palette, East Valley place names, same-day scorpion/termite positioning, child-safety origin story, wall-void treatment process, and direct 602-380-8869 CTA.
