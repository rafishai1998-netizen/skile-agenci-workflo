# Strategy

## Audience

East Valley homeowners and parents who want fast relief from scorpions, termites, roaches, roof rats, mosquitoes, bees, weeds, and other Arizona pest issues. Their real concern is not just bugs; it is feeling safe inside their own home.

## Primary promise

Same-day pest control from a local father-son team built around protecting families from Arizona scorpions and pests.

## Conversion action

Primary: call 602-380-8869.

Secondary: send a request through a short contact form with pest type, ZIP code, and preferred contact method.

## Page story

1. Open with protection, speed, and local specificity.
2. Show the actual Richardson logo, real technician photo, service area, license, and Saturday availability.
3. Explain the scorpion origin story early enough that the brand feels human and memorable.
4. Turn service complexity into easy cards organized by homeowner intent.
5. Show the three-step process and the specific wall-void treatment mechanics.
6. Close with safety, family, and same-day CTA.

## Design strategy

Use a dark charcoal base with amber scorpion accents. Avoid generic green pest-control cliches. Make the page feel like a protective shield for the home: bold angles, amber lines, card depth, and a recurring stinger motif. Use real Richardson photos instead of stock. The design should be confident but not macho; the emotional center is family safety.
