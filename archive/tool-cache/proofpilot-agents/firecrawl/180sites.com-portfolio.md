Frequently Asked Design Questions

###### What's the difference between a template site and a custom site?

Our template sites are just as beautiful, effective, and optimized as our custom sites are. The only difference is that a custom site is going to be a completely unique one-of-a-kind website creation. In terms of SEO, aesthetics, functionality, and conversion optimization, all of these tactics are going to be applied the same across our template sites and our custom sites; in this regard, there is no difference. If you have a very unique brand or a very specific design that you are looking to achieve, then a fully custom site is the best solution for you. If you love the way our template sites look and you want to save some money, then our template packages are the best option for you. If your business is in an industry that we do not have a template showcased for, reach out to us and see if we can build a template for your industry.

###### Why are your template sites so cheap?

All of our packages start with a template design. This is the most affordable way to go. The reason that we’re able to offer these at such an affordable price is that we have optimized our systems and processes to create our templated sites as efficiently as possible. We have created several beautiful and highly effective designs for many different industries. You get to pick your favorite and then we use that design concept to build out your site. This saves us time and saves you money. If you want to get a fully custom site, you may pay a one-time upgrade cost to do so.

###### Can you build me a fully custom Site?

Yes, you can upgrade any package to be a fully custom design for a one time upgrade cost of $2,500.

###### If I show you a website that I love can you make something similar?

Heck yeah, we can build pretty much anything that you want, no matter the design or functionality; our developers will most certainly be able to assist you.
