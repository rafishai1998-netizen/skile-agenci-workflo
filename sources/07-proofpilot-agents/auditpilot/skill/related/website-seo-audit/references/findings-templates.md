# Findings Templates

Templates for writing audit findings, recommendations, and key insights.

## Key Findings Section

### What's Working Well

Template for positive findings:

```
1. [Specific element] [quantifiable result or evidence]
   Example: "Strong mobile responsiveness with 9/10 score and no usability issues"

2. [Trust/conversion element] [why it matters]
   Example: "Owner photo prominently displayed on homepage builds immediate trust"

3. [SEO/technical element] [competitive advantage]
   Example: "Google Business Profile fully optimized with 127 reviews averaging 4.9 stars"
```

**Good examples:**
- "Custom video testimonials from real customers differentiate from competitors using stock photos"
- "Click-to-call phone number in header makes mobile conversion seamless"
- "Comprehensive service pages averaging 800+ words rank for 15+ keywords each"
- "Local Falcon shows green (#1-3) across entire core service area for primary keyword"

**Bad examples (too generic):**
- "Website looks nice"
- "They have reviews"
- "SEO is okay"

### Critical Issues to Fix

Template for problems:

```
1. [Specific problem] → [Business impact]
   Example: "38 blog posts about logistics company have nothing to do with electrical services → Google may penalize for spam content"

2. [Missing element] → [Lost opportunity]
   Example: "Zero location pages for cities served → Invisible for 'electrician mesa', 'electrician scottsdale' searches capturing 2,400 monthly searches"

3. [Competitive gap] → [Revenue impact]
   Example: "Competitor Code 3 Junk Removal ranks #1-3 across entire service area while client averages position 14 → Losing estimated 50+ calls per month"
```

**Priority order:** Most urgent first. Lead with what's costing the most money.

**Good examples:**
- "No Google Business Profile posts in 6 months → GBP algorithm penalizes inactive profiles"
- "Form requires 8 fields including company name and address → Studies show each additional field reduces conversions 10%"
- "Page speed score of 23/100 with 6.2 second load time → 40% of visitors abandon sites that take longer than 3 seconds"
- "Meta descriptions missing on 12 of 15 pages → Google generates random snippets reducing click-through rate"

**Bad examples (too vague):**
- "SEO needs work"
- "Website is slow"
- "Need more content"

---

### Quick Wins

Template for easy fixes with high impact:

```
1. [Simple action] → [Expected result]
   Example: "Add phone number to mobile header → Immediate increase in call-through rate"

2. [One-time fix] → [Ongoing benefit]
   Example: "Add LocalBusiness schema markup → Improved rich results and AI discoverability"

3. [Low-cost change] → [Competitive advantage]
   Example: "Display Google review count '127 Five-Star Reviews' on homepage → Instant trust signal"
```

**Criteria for Quick Wins:**
- Can be done in 1-2 hours
- Doesn't require major development
- Has measurable impact
- Improves score in at least one category

**Good examples:**
- "Add license number and insurance badge to homepage footer"
- "Write unique meta descriptions for top 5 service pages"
- "Enable click-to-call on mobile phone numbers"
- "Add 'Satisfaction Guaranteed' badge to hero section"
- "Compress images to improve page speed"
- "Add FAQ section matching People Also Ask"
- "Claim and verify Bing Places listing"
- "Respond to all unanswered Google reviews"

### Pages Needed But Missing

Template:

```
1. [Page type] — [Target keyword] ([Search volume])
   Example: "Panel Upgrade Service Page — 'panel upgrade mesa' (320 monthly searches)"

2. [Location page] — [Geographic coverage]
   Example: "Gilbert Location Page — 'electrician gilbert az' (590 monthly searches)"

3. [Service+Location combo] — [High-intent keyword]
   Example: "EV Charger Installation Scottsdale — 'ev charger installation scottsdale' (170 monthly searches)"
```

**Categories to check:**

**Service Pages:**
- Do they have a page for each GBP category?
- Do they have pages for specialty services?
- Do they have pages for high-ticket services?

**Location Pages:**
- Do they have a page for each city served?
- Do they have pages for neighborhoods in major cities?
- Do they have county-level pages for broader searches?

**Combo Pages (Service + Location):**
- Primary service + each city
- High-ticket service + affluent areas

**Supporting Content:**
- Pricing/cost guide pages
- Comparison pages (vs. DIY, vs. competitors)
- "Best [service] in [city]" pages (for AI visibility)
- FAQ page with schema markup

## Keyword Gap Template

```
| Keyword | Monthly Volume | Competitor Ranking | Client Position | Opportunity |
|---------|----------------|--------------------|-----------------|-------------|
| [keyword] | [volume] | [position] | Not ranking | [revenue estimate] |
```

**Opportunity calculation:**
- 1% CTR assumption for position 1
- Estimate leads from traffic
- Apply average job value
- Example: 500 searches x 1% CTR x 10% conversion x $500 avg job = $250/month potential

## Notes on Writing Style

**Be specific:** Include numbers, names, and evidence.

**Be direct:** Lead with the finding, not the explanation.

**Compare to competitors:** Context makes problems tangible.

**Quantify impact:** Revenue, leads, rankings, whatever matters to them.

**Avoid jargon:** Write so a business owner understands without SEO expertise.

**Use their language:** Reference their services, cities, and business name.
