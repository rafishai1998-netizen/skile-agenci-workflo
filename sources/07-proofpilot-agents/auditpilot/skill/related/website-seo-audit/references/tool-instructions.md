## BrightLocal Local SEO Audit

This audit gives you on-page SEO, links, citations, and overall local SEO health.

### How to Run

1. Log into BrightLocal
2. Go to **Local SEO Audit** (under Reports)
3. Enter the business website URL
4. Enter the business name and location
5. Run the audit and wait for results

### What the Audit Shows

| Section | What It Measures |
|---------|-----------------|
| On-Page SEO | Title tags, meta descriptions, H1 tags, schema markup |
| Link Profile | Backlinks, referring domains, link quality |
| Citations | NAP consistency, citation count, missing citations |
| Overall Score | Combined local SEO health metric |

### Extracting Data

**On-Page SEO Score:**
- Navigate to On-Page section
- Note the score out of 100
- List specific issues found (missing descriptions, duplicate titles, etc.)

**Link Profile:**
- Navigate to Links section
- Note referring domain count
- Check link quality distribution

**Citations:**
- Navigate to Citations section
- Note total citation count
- List any NAP inconsistencies with specific platforms

## BrightLocal GBP Audit

Separate audit focused on Google Business Profile health and competitor analysis.

### How to Run

1. In BrightLocal, go to **Google Business Profile Audit**
2. Enter the business name or connect their GBP
3. Select the primary keyword
4. Run the audit

### What It Shows

| Data Point | Why It Matters |
|------------|----------------|
| GBP Completeness | Profile filled out, photos, posts, Q&A |
| Competitor Rankings | Who ranks for their keywords in local pack |
| Main Keywords | Keywords the GBP is appearing for |
| Review Analysis | Review count, rating, response rate |

### Key Data to Extract

1. **Profile Completeness:** What percentage? What's missing?
2. **Photo Count:** How many photos uploaded?
3. **Post Frequency:** When was the last post?
4. **Review Count & Rating:** Compare to competitors
5. **Categories:** Primary and secondary categories set?
6. **Services/Products:** Are they listed?
7. **Q&A:** Any questions answered?

### Competitor Data

Note the top 3 competitors shown in the audit:
- Their review counts
- Their ratings
- Their ranking positions
- What they have that the client doesn't

## Local Falcon Heatmaps

Visual grid showing local pack rankings across the service area.

### How to Run

1. Open Local Falcon
2. Enter business name
3. Enter primary keyword (e.g., "electrician", "plumber")
4. Set grid center to business address
5. Set grid size to cover service area (typically 15-25 mile radius)
6. Select grid density (7x7 or 9x9 recommended)
7. Run scan
8. **Screenshot the heatmap for the audit**

### Interpreting Colors

| Color | Position | Meaning |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Green | #1-3 | In the 3-pack. Getting calls. |
| Yellow | #4-7 | Close but not visible. Missing calls. |
| Red | #8-20 | Not ranking. Competitors getting calls. |
| Gray | 20+ | Completely invisible in that area. |

### What to Document

1. **Overall pattern:** Mostly green? Mostly red?
2. **Average position:** What's the number in the center?
3. **Strong areas:** Where are they ranking well?
4. **Weak areas:** Where are they invisible?
5. **Competitor names:** Who shows instead in red areas?

### Multiple Scans

Run at least 2 heatmaps:
1. **Primary keyword:** Their main service (e.g., "electrician mesa")
2. **Secondary keyword:** A specialty service (e.g., "ev charger installation mesa")

This shows whether their rankings are consistent or service-specific.

## Ahrefs/SEMrush Analysis

Keyword rankings, traffic estimates, and competitor gaps.

### Key Reports to Pull

**Organic Keywords:**
- Total keywords ranking
- Top 10 rankings
- Positions 11-20 (opportunity keywords)

**Traffic Estimate:**
- Monthly organic traffic
- Traffic trend over 6-12 months
- Traffic by page (which pages drive traffic?)

**Top Pages:**
- Which pages get the most traffic?
- Are service pages ranking or just blog posts?

**Keyword Gap Analysis:**
- Compare to top 3 competitors
- Find keywords competitors rank for that client doesn't
- Focus on commercial intent keywords

### Data to Extract

| Metric | What to Note |
|--------|--------------|
| Monthly Traffic | Number and 6-month trend |
| Top Keywords | List top 10 with positions |
| Traffic Sources | Organic vs. branded vs. blog |
| Gap Keywords | Top 10 competitor keywords client lacks |

## Manual Website Review

Trust, conversion, and content quality require human review.

### Trust Review Checklist

Open the website and look for:

- [ ] Guarantee or risk reversal prominently displayed
- [ ] Google reviews widget or testimonials section
- [ ] Star rating and review count visible
- [ ] License number displayed (not just "Licensed")
- [ ] Insurance mentioned
- [ ] BBB badge or other trust badges
- [ ] Owner photo on homepage
- [ ] Team page with real photos
- [ ] About page with company story
- [ ] Mission or values statement
- [ ] Community involvement mentioned

### Conversion Review Checklist

- [ ] Phone number in header
- [ ] Phone number is click-to-call on mobile
- [ ] "Get Quote" or CTA button visible above fold
- [ ] CTA on every page
- [ ] Contact form present and working
- [ ] Form is simple (not too many fields)
- [ ] Mobile site loads properly
- [ ] Navigation works on mobile
- [ ] Page loads quickly (under 3 seconds)
- [ ] No broken links or images
- [ ] Clear service offerings
- [ ] Pricing indicators if appropriate

### Content Review Checklist

- [ ] Homepage has substantial content (not just images)
- [ ] Dedicated page for each main service
- [ ] Location pages for cities served
- [ ] Blog exists
- [ ] Blog content is relevant to their services
- [ ] Blog content is not obviously AI-generated spam
- [ ] FAQs section exists
- [ ] FAQs answer real customer questions
- [ ] Service pages have 500+ words
- [ ] Content is unique (not duplicated across pages)

### Technical SEO Checklist

Use browser developer tools or SEO extension:

- [ ] Unique meta title on homepage
- [ ] Meta title includes primary keyword and city
- [ ] Unique meta description
- [ ] H1 tag on homepage
- [ ] Schema markup present (check with Schema Validator)
- [ ] XML sitemap exists (/sitemap.xml)
- [ ] Robots.txt not blocking important pages
- [ ] SSL certificate active (https)
- [ ] No duplicate content issues

## Screenshot Checklist

Include these screenshots in the audit:

1. **Local Falcon heatmap** - Primary keyword
2. **Local Falcon heatmap** - Secondary keyword (if different results)
3. **BrightLocal overview** - Overall scores
4. **Competitor comparison** - Rankings vs. top 3
5. **PageSpeed Insights** - Mobile score
6. **Homepage above-fold** - Shows trust signals (or lack of)
7. **Mobile homepage** - Shows mobile experience
