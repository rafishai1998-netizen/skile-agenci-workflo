# AuditPilot Strategic Brain

Load on EVERY audit. 8 strategic dimensions that no automated tool checks.

## Core Principle
AuditPilot answers: "Why is this business invisible online?" not "What technical issues exist?"

## Dimension 1: Content Authenticity
Scrape same service across 3+ cities, strip city names, compare. >80% identical = templated. Check for wrong city names, "City, AZ, AZ" bugs. All blog posts same day = mass generated. Perfect city symmetry = programmatic.

Report as: "We compared your drain cleaning page for Mesa vs Scottsdale vs Chandler. After removing city names, 92% was identical. Google's Helpful Content Update targets this."

## Dimension 2: Built for Robots vs Humans
Navigation test: "Services" category or just city names? Page count vs business size (345 pages for solo plumber = red flag). URL structure (/components/about = developer leak). Content cannibalization (3+ pages per city targeting same keyword).

## Dimension 3: Design & UX as Ranking Signal
Homepage 5-second test: value prop or just "Plumber in Mesa"? Real photos or stock? Same image across all pages? Service pages: walls of text or real content with images/pricing? Review count shown or just static star image?

## Dimension 4: Trust & Credibility Depth
License NUMBER visible (not just "licensed")? Live review widget or static image? Actual review count or just logos? Real team photos? Physical address? Professional email?

## Dimension 5: Conversion Architecture
CTA above fold? Phone in header? Booking widget or just "call us"? Form friction? Location page links correct or all pointing to one city?

## Dimension 6: Content Strategy Coherence
Blog: organic dates or batch published? Service page overlap. Location pages: local knowledge or tourism padding? Depth vs width.

## Dimension 7: Competitive Context
Compare PAGE QUALITY not just count. Market sizing based on SEARCH VOLUME not pages.

## Dimension 8: The Verdict Framework
End every audit with a STORY, not a list. Template: "Your site has [STRENGTH], but it's being undermined by [ROOT CAUSE]."

## Detection Checklist
- Same service across 3+ cities text comparison
- Total pages vs logical count
- Blog dates for batch patterns
- Navigation: service-first or city-first
- Copy-paste errors (wrong city, double state)
- Location page links to correct city
- Homepage impression (photo, reviews, value prop)
- Review presentation (widget vs static)
- Phone consistency
- Developer path artifacts (/components/)
