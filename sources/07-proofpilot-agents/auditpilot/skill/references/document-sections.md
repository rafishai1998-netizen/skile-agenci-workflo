# AuditPilot Document Section Details

## HTML Proposal Mode (Sales Opportunities)

Use this when the deliverable is a short, premium HTML proposal instead of a long .docx audit.
Structure the narrative in this order:
1. Current State
2. Competitive Threat
3. Revenue Opportunity
4. Strategic Solution

Recommended section mapping:
- Hero Cover: thum.io screenshot, headline, biggest revenue number
- Executive Summary: market capture, dollars left on the table, competitors winning now
- Revenue Leaks: Technical, Content, Authority, UX cards with current and potential state
- Competitive Reality Check: prospect vs top competitor, plus top competitors table
- Striking Distance: page-two keywords with inline SVG distribution visual
- Revenue Projection: traffic lift, lead lift, revenue lift, cost of inaction
- Authority Profile: referring domains, anchor mix, trust gap
- Local Snapshot: geo-grid visibility when local intent exists
- 30/60/90 Day Roadmap: Fix Revenue Leaks, Content Velocity, Authority Building
- CTA: clean next step

Math to include when possible:
- Unrealized revenue proxy from striking-distance keywords
- Ad spend equivalent / traffic value
- Competitive traffic-value gap
- Cost of inaction

## Section 04: Who Is Outranking You
THE data section. Uses REAL DataForSEO data.

Competitor Overview Table: Pages, Reviews, Rating, Total Ranked Keywords.
Keyword Rankings Side-by-Side: Your top keywords vs competitor keywords with volume, CPC, position.
The GAP: keywords competitors have that you don't.
Market Sizing: "X monthly searches in your market."

## Section 07: Keywords and Opportunities

A) Money Keywords: High CPC ($30+) transactional table. Sort by CPC descending.
B) Long-Tail: Service+city combos. Group by service category.
C) Missing Pages: Table with target keyword, volume, avg job value.

## Section 08: Revenue Impact and ROI

NO prices. Just the opportunity.

A) What SEO Could Bring: Conservative/Realistic/Growth scenarios with traffic, leads, jobs, revenue.
B) Google Ads Equivalent: "Buying this traffic = $X,XXX/month" using real CPC data.
C) Lifetime Customer Value: $47 lead becomes $5,000-$15,000 customer.
D) Cost of Waiting: Every month = $X lost. Competitors get stronger. SEO compounds.
