# 90-Day Posts Calendar

## Why posts matter

GBP posts support engagement and give the profile fresh local activity. They also let the business reinforce key services, offers, and local relevance directly in the profile.

## Cadence

Recommended cadence:
- Weeks 1 to 4: 2 posts per week for launch momentum
- Weeks 5 to 12: 1 post per week
- Total first 90 days: 10 to 14 posts

## Post types

Rotate across these four buckets:
- Offer
- Update
- Event
- Product spotlight

## Post formula

Every post should have:
1. one-line hook
2. 2 to 3 sentences of context
3. one clear CTA

Keep most posts under 300 characters for strong mobile readability.

## 90-day default pattern

| Week | Post type | Topic |
|------|-----------|-------|
| 1 | Update | Business overview and differentiator |
| 1 | Offer | Lead-magnet offer or first-call promotion |
| 2 | Product | Flagship service 1 |
| 2 | Update | Team or owner spotlight |
| 3 | Product | Flagship service 2 |
| 3 | Offer | Seasonal reminder |
| 4 | Update | Community or local proof item |
| 5 | Product | Flagship service 3 |
| 6 | Offer | Limited-time promotion |
| 7 | Event | Community event or local milestone |
| 8 | Product | Flagship service 4 |
| 9 | Update | Certification, press mention, or proof asset |
| 10 | Offer | Seasonal offer |
| 11 | Product | Flagship service 5 |
| 12 | Update | Customer win or 90-day recap |

## Local-intent guidance

Local references improve click-through and relevance.

Use:
- neighborhood names
- city names
- recognizable landmarks
- recently completed jobs or service calls by area

## Do not

- repeat the same post inside 30 days
- post without a CTA destination
- post without a relevant image
- publish posts that link to non-existent pages
- overload the copy with emojis or generic fluff
