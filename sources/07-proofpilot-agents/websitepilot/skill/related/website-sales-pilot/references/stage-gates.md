# WebsiteSalesPilot Stage Gates

## 1. Lead qualification
Entry:
- a domain exists
- the business has a clear primary service and market
- the lead is worth a website-focused sales pass

Exit:
- root domain confirmed
- primary service confirmed
- primary city/market confirmed
- engagement tier chosen: light, standard, or full

## 2. Sales audit
Entry:
- domain and market are confirmed
- audit mode is selected

Exit:
- current visibility story is clear
- ranking/indexing/competitor evidence exists
- top 3-5 pain points are written plainly

## 3. Strategy blueprint
Entry:
- audit findings exist
- the core business offer is clear

Exit:
- homepage thesis is defined
- recommended page systems are prioritized
- first 90-day roadmap is clear enough to build from

## 4. Demo homepage brief
Entry:
- audit and strategy both exist

Exit:
- a structured homepage brief exists
- the brief is compressed into an AutoPilot-ready notes block
- the CTA and audience are explicit

## 5. Demo generation
Entry:
- AutoPilot connectivity verified
- homepage brief approved internally

Exit:
- preview URL exists or a clear blocker is logged
- output is visually reviewed

## 6. Sales bundle delivery
Entry:
- final artifacts exist

Exit:
- Matthew gets the right amount of material for the lead tier
- next action is obvious
