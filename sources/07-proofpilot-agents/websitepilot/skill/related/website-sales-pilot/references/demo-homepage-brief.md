# Demo Homepage Brief

Use this structure before launching AutoPilot with `page_type=homepage`.

## Required fields
- Business name
- Domain
- Primary service
- Primary market
- Target keyword or theme
- Primary audience
- Primary CTA

## Sales context
- Main pain the audit proved
- Main competitor or market pressure
- Main missed opportunity
- What the current site fails to communicate

## Strategic angle
- Homepage thesis in one sentence
- 3 differentiators to emphasize
- Trust assets or proof to surface
- Priority sections the homepage must include

## Notes block for AutoPilot
Compress the brief into a concise notes block with these subheadings:
- Pain
- Opportunity
- Positioning
- Proof
- CTA
- Design cues

## Example notes block
Pain: The current site looks credible enough but does not rank or convert, and competitors with deeper service/location coverage are taking the calls.
Opportunity: Position the homepage as the fastest path from invisible website to booked local jobs.
Positioning: ProofPilot is the specialist team for home service websites and local SEO, not a generic marketing shop.
Proof: Use local SEO wins, conversion improvements, and operational clarity.
CTA: Push a clear estimate or strategy-call CTA above the fold and throughout the page.
Design cues: Clean premium ProofPilot look, strong contrast, strategic stats, trust rows, and proof-heavy sections.
