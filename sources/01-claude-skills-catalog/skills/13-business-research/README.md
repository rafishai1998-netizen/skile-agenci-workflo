# Business Research

> Market research, deep research, neural search, investor materials, and outreach strategies for business intelligence and fundraising.

## When to Use These Skills
- Conducting competitive analysis or market sizing research
- Performing deep, multi-source research on a topic or industry
- Preparing investor decks, one-pagers, or fundraising materials
- Building targeted outreach campaigns for investors or partners

## Quick Reference

| Skill | Difficulty | Trigger Phrase | Best For |
|-------|-----------|----------------|----------|
| deep-research | Intermediate | `/deep-research` | Thorough multi-source research synthesis |
| market-research | Intermediate | `/market-research` | Competitive analysis and market sizing |

## Start Here
If you're new to this category, start with **deep-research** for thorough multi-source investigation or **market-research** for competitive and market analysis. These are the most broadly applicable research skills.

## Skill Relationships
**deep-research** is the most comprehensive research skill -- it synthesizes information from multiple sources into a structured report. **market-research** focuses specifically on competitive landscape, market sizing, and industry dynamics. **neural-search** provides an advanced search layer that both deep-research and market-research can leverage for semantic discovery. The investor skills form a pipeline: **market-research** feeds into **investor-deck** and **investor-one-pager** for materials, which then feed into **investor-outreach** for distribution strategy. **business-intelligence** cuts across all skills by providing data-driven analysis frameworks.
