```
EBITDA = Gross Profit - OpEx

TAX RESERVE (29.8% of EBITDA)
├── Self-Employment Tax (15.3%)
├── Federal Income Tax (12%)
├── Arizona State Tax (2.5%)
└── TOTAL TAX RESERVE

INCOME AFTER TAX = EBITDA - Tax Reserve

OWNER COMPENSATION
├── Owner Draw
├── SEP-IRA
└── TOTAL OWNER PAY

NET PROFIT = Income After Tax - Owner Pay
```

## Spreadsheet Columns

| Column | Content |
|--------|---------|
| A | Category |
| B | Current Month Actual |
| C | Prior Month Actual |
| D | Change (B - C) |
| E | % of Revenue (B / Total Revenue) |
| F | Notes |

## Key Metrics to Display

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Gross Margin | Gross Profit / Revenue | 62-68% |
| EBITDA Margin | EBITDA / Revenue | 55-65% |
| COGS % | COGS / Revenue | 32-38% |
| OpEx % | OpEx / Revenue | 4-6% |
| After-Tax % | After Tax Income / Revenue | 40-45% |
| Net Margin | Net Profit / Revenue | Positive |
