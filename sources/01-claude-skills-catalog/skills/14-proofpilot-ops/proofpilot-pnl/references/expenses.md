## Exclude from P&L

These items should NOT be included in operating expenses:

| Type | Examples | Bank Patterns |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Personal purchases | Electronics, personal items | BEST BUY, AMAZON (unless business) |
| Debt payoffs | Credit card payments | AMEX EPAYMENT |
| Client-reimbursed | Items billed back to clients | Vistaprint (confirm with user) |
| Internal transfers | Moving money between accounts | Transfer between your Mercury accounts |
| Failed transactions | Status = "Failed" | Check Status column |
