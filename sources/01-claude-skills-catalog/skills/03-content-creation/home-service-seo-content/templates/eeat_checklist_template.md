# E-E-A-T Checklist for [Client Name]

## Experience
- [ ] Include author bio with years of experience, certifications, and a headshot.
- [ ] Add case studies with before-and-after photos of local jobs.
- [ ] Use first-person language (e.g., "In my 20 years as a plumber...").
- [ ] Feature video testimonials from real customers in [City].

## Expertise
- [ ] Create a comprehensive guide that answers common customer questions.
- [ ] Build out a detailed FAQ section addressing at least 10-15 related questions.
- [ ] Use precise, authoritative language.
- [ ] Cite reputable sources (e.g., building codes, manufacturer specifications).

## Authoritativeness
- [ ] Add a section for licenses, certifications, and awards.
- [ ] Include logos of local associations or publications the business is featured in.
- [ ] Link to the business's BBB profile.
- [ ] Mention any local events or sponsorships.

## Trustworthiness
- [ ] Ensure clear pricing and contact information is visible.
- [ ] Add a section on guarantees or warranties.
- [ ] Embed positive Google or Yelp reviews directly in the content.
- [ ] Verify that the website has HTTPS and a clear privacy policy.
