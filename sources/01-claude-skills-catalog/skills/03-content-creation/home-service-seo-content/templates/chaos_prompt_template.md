# Chaos Prompt for Humanization

This prompt is designed to be appended to a content generation request to add a layer of human-like imperfection and chaos. It also enforces the Anti-AI Writing Style Guide. It is based on research from Reddit communities on how to make AI-generated content sound more human and less predictable.

## The Prompt

---

**Humanization Instructions:**

Now, take the article you have generated and apply the following “chaos” edits to make it sound more human and less like polished AI output. Follow these critical instructions:

*   **Perplexity & Predictability Control**:
    *   Deliberately choose 1-2 unexpected, creative word alternatives instead of obvious ones.
    *   Include at least one colloquialism or region-specific expression relevant to the client’s service area.
    *   Add one subtle imperfection, such as a minor redundancy or a natural speech pattern (e.g., “So, what does that mean for you? Well, it means…”).

*   **Burstiness & Sentence Variation**:
    *   Ensure there is dramatic sentence length variation. Find a paragraph with uniform sentence length and rewrite it to include at least one very short sentence (3-5 words) and one longer, more complex sentence (20+ words).
    *   Include one intentional sentence fragment where it feels natural.
    *   Use one parenthetical aside for an authentic human flow.

*   **Emotional Intelligence & Human Touch**:
    *   Add one personal opinion or qualifier (e.g., “In our experience,” “We believe,” “It seems to us”).
    *   Include one conversational transition between paragraphs that feels less mechanical (e.g., “But here’s the thing…,” “So, what’s the bottom line?”).

*   **Structural Pattern Disruption**:
    *   Break one conventional grammar rule in a natural way (e.g., starting a sentence with “And” or “But”).

**Final Requirement**: The goal is to subtly rough up the edges of the AI-generated text, not to make it sloppy. The final output should feel like it was written by a real, knowledgeable, and slightly busy expert, not a perfect machine.
