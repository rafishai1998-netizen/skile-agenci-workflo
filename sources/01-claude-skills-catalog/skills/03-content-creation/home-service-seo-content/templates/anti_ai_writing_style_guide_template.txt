'''# Anti-AI Writing Style Guide

## Core Principles

-   **Clarity Over Complexity**: Use clear, simple language. Your goal is to be understood, not to sound smart.
-   **Be Spartan and Informative**: Every word should serve a purpose. If it doesn't add value, cut it.
-   **Active Voice Always**: Avoid passive voice. It weakens your writing and creates distance.

## The Rules

1.  **Use short, impactful sentences.**
2.  **Address the reader directly** with "you" and "your".
3.  **Use data and examples** to support all claims.
4.  **AVOID em dashes (—)**. Use periods or commas instead.
5.  **AVOID semicolons (;)** unless absolutely necessary for complex lists.
6.  **AVOID colons (:)** for simple introductions. Rephrase the sentence.
7.  **AVOID constructions like** "...not just this, but also this".
8.  **AVOID metaphors and clichés.**
9.  **AVOID generalizations.** Be specific.
10. **AVOID common setup language** like "in conclusion" or "in summary".
11. **AVOID unnecessary adjectives and adverbs.**
12. **AVOID hashtags, asterisks, and markdown** in final copy.
