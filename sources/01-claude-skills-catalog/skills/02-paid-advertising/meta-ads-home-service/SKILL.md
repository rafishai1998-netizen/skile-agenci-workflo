# Meta Ads for Home Service Businesses

Complete system for creating Meta/Facebook ad campaigns for home service clients.
Built from $600K+ in tested ad spend across 700+ home service businesses, combined
with ProofPilot's real campaign data ($3-6 CPL on quiz funnels, 8.75x ROAS).

## Core Philosophy

**The creative IS the targeting.** Meta's algorithm handles audience selection.
Interest targeting is largely irrelevant. The quality of your ad creative — what
you say, how you look, and where you film — is what finds the customer.

## Priority Order for Every Client

1. **Video Ads** — Owner intro videos are the #1 proven format. Always start here.
2. **Static Ads** — Before/after images, offer graphics. Pair with video for testing.
3. **Testimonial Videos** — Add after first 30 days once jobs are completed.

## Full Strategy Workflow

When building a complete Meta ads strategy for a new client:

### Step 1: Client Discovery (Ask These Questions)

1. **Business basics:** Company name, owner name, location/service area, years in business
2. **Services:** Primary service to advertise, average ticket price, sales cycle length
3. **Differentiators:** What makes them different? (licensed, insured, years experience, specialization, certifications, warranty, reviews count)
4. **Current marketing:** Any past Meta ads? Current monthly marketing budget? CRM in use?
5. **Offer:** What can they offer? (Free inspection, $X off, free consultation)
6. **Content assets:** Can the owner film video? Do they have before/after photos? Testimonials?
7. **Automation:** Do they have auto-text/email on lead submission? Speed to lead?
8. **Quick-close or long-close?** Window cleaning = quick. Remodeling = long. This changes expectations.

### Step 2: Build the Creative Package

Generate the following deliverables:

**Video Ads (Priority 1):**
- 3-5 owner intro video scripts using the 6-Step Framework (see [video-scripts.md](resources/video-scripts.md))
- 1 testimonial interview framework
- Filming checklist (what to wear, where to film, equipment)

**Static Ads (Priority 2):**
- 3-5 static ad concepts (see [static-ads.md](resources/static-ads.md))
- Before/after layouts, offer graphics, trust-signal images
- Pair with video ads in the same campaign for testing

**Ad Copy:**
- Primary text, headline, and description for each creative
- See [ad-copy-templates.md](resources/ad-copy-templates.md)

### Step 3: Campaign Architecture

Follow the complete setup guide in [campaign-setup.md](resources/campaign-setup.md).

Quick summary:
- **Objective:** Leads (lead form or quiz funnel traffic)
- **Budget:** CBO, start $40-50/day minimum
- **Targeting:** Broad + location only. Current city only. Uncheck "reach more people"
- **Creatives:** Mix of 3-5 video + 3-5 static in one campaign
- **CTA:** "Get Quote" (outperforms all other CTAs)
- **Lead capture:** Quiz funnel for high-ticket ($1K+), Meta lead form for low-ticket

### Step 4: Lead Funnel Decision

| Average Ticket | Recommended | Why |
|---------------|-------------|-----|
| Under $500 | Meta Lead Form | Low friction, fast conversion, volume play |
| $500 - $1,000 | Meta Lead Form + friction question | Filter for quality without losing volume |
| Over $1,000 | Quiz Funnel (external) | Pre-qualifies leads, tiered offers, dramatically better quality |

For quiz funnel specs, see [quiz-funnel-spec.md](resources/quiz-funnel-spec.md).

### Step 5: Automation Requirements (Non-Negotiable)

Every client MUST have before launching ads:
1. **Instant auto-text** when lead submits (< 60 seconds)
2. **Voicemail drop** on submission
3. **5-text follow-up sequence** if no response
4. **CRM integration** with lead source tracking
5. **Someone answering the phone** or dedicated setter calling leads daily

Without these, the ads will "not work" — but it's a systems failure, not a platform failure.

### Step 6: Reporting Setup

Use the ProofPilot marketing tracking sheet format. Track:

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Spend** | Total ad spend | Per client budget |
| **Leads** | Form/quiz submissions | Volume varies by budget |
| **CPL** | Spend / leads | $15-30 for most trades |
| **Appointments** | Booked from leads | 20-30% of leads |
| **Cost Per Appointment** | Spend / appointments | Primary metric |
| **Closed Jobs** | Appointments that closed | 25-40% of appointments |
| **Revenue** | Total from closed jobs | Track by source |
| **CAC** | Spend / closed customers | The metric that matters |
| **ROAS** | Revenue / spend | Target 3-5x after 30 days |

See [reporting.md](resources/reporting.md) for full framework.

## Key Rules (Burned Into Every Campaign)

1. **Never run only 1 creative.** Minimum 4, ideally 6-8 per campaign.
2. **Dollar amounts beat percentages.** "$150 off" outperforms "15% off" even when 15% is more.
3. **Location callout stops the scroll.** Always start with city/neighborhood name.
4. **Subtitles on every video.** 85% watch without sound.
5. **Videos under 45 seconds.** 30 seconds ideal, 60 max.
6. **Branded polo + truck = trust.** No white t-shirts, no messy backgrounds.
7. **Don't kill campaigns too early.** Long-close industries need weeks. Let the pipeline fill.
8. **CPL is a vanity metric.** Cost per appointment and CAC are what matter.
9. **Scale in $50/day increments.** Every 2 weeks if profitable.
10. **Creative fatigue is solved by more creative.** Not by pausing campaigns.

## Account Hygiene & Ban Prevention

- Use a dedicated Business Manager for each client (not their personal account)
- Set up a backup ad account in the same Business Manager
- Never use personal Facebook profiles to run ads — get banned and you lose everything
- Diversify payment methods (2 cards minimum per account)
- Don't edit ads during learning phase (first 50 conversions or 7 days)
- If account gets restricted: appeal immediately, have a backup account ready
- Keep ad account clean: archive old campaigns, don't leave broken ads running
