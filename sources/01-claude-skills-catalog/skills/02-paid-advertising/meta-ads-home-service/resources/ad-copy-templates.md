# Ad Copy Templates for Home Service Meta Ads

The ad copy (primary text, headline, description) wraps around your video or image.
It reinforces the message and catches people who read before watching.

---

## Primary Text Templates

### Template 1: Direct Offer (Most Common)

```
[CITY] homeowners — [COMPANY NAME] is offering [OFFER] this month only.

[1-2 sentences about the service and why it matters]

[Trust point] | [Trust point] | [Trust point]

Click "Get Quote" to claim your spot. Limited availability.
```

**Example (Electrician):**
```
Orange County homeowners — All Thingz Electric is offering $250 off EV charger
installation this month only.

Stop driving to public chargers. We install Level 2 home chargers with a full
load calculation on your panel so everything runs safely.

Licensed C-10 Contractor | 500+ Installs | 5-Year Warranty

Click "Get Quote" to claim your spot. Limited availability.
```

### Template 2: Problem-Solution

```
If your home was built before [YEAR], your [SYSTEM] could be [PROBLEM].

[1-2 sentences explaining the risk]

[COMPANY NAME] offers [FREE SERVICE / OFFER] to [CITY] homeowners this month.

[Trust points on one line]

Tap below to get started.
```

```
If your home was built before 1990, your electrical panel could be a fire hazard.

```

This month: [OFFER]

## Copy Rules

1. **Match the video/image offer.** If the video says "$150 off," the copy must say "$150 off" too.
2. **Dollar amounts > percentages.** Always use flat dollar amounts in copy.
3. **Include the city/area name.** Reinforces local targeting.
4. **3 trust points on one line.** Separated by pipes or bullets.
5. **End with clear CTA.** "Click Get Quote" / "Tap below" / "Limited spots."
6. **No wall of text.** 4-6 lines max. Short paragraphs with line breaks.
7. **No emojis in primary text.** Keep it professional for home service.
8. **Mention the offer in copy even if it's in the video.** Not everyone watches.

## Spanish-Language Ad Copy Framework

For markets with significant Spanish-speaking populations (e.g., Saiyan Electric in Downey/Edison area):

### Approach
- Translate the SAME winning ad copy — don't create new angles
- Use a native speaker for translation (not Google Translate)
- Run as separate ad set within the same campaign
- Target same geography with Spanish language setting

### Template
```
Propietarios de [CIUDAD] — [NOMBRE DE EMPRESA] ofrece [OFERTA] solo este mes.

[1-2 oraciones sobre el servicio]

[Punto de confianza] | [Punto de confianza] | [Punto de confianza]

Haga clic en "Obtener Cotizacion" para reservar su lugar.
```
