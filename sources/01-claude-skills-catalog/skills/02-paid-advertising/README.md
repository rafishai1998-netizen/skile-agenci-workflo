# Paid Advertising

> Multi-platform ad auditing, budget optimization, creative analysis, and strategic planning across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, Microsoft, and YouTube.

## When to Use These Skills
- Auditing ad account performance across one or more platforms
- Optimizing ad spend, budgets, and bidding strategies
- Reviewing ad creatives, landing pages, or competitor campaigns
- Building a cross-platform paid media strategy

## Start Here
If you're new to this category, start with **ads** -- it's the most general-purpose and will route you to platform-specific auditors based on your needs. For a comprehensive multi-platform review, use **ads-audit**.

## Skill Relationships
The **ads** skill is the central router that dispatches to platform-specific skills (**ads-google**, **ads-meta**, **ads-linkedin**, **ads-tiktok**, **ads-microsoft**, **ads-youtube**). The **ads-audit** skill orchestrates across platforms for a unified report. Supporting skills like **ads-creative**, **ads-budget**, and **ads-landing** analyze specific dimensions that cut across all platforms. **ads-competitor** feeds intelligence into **ads-plan** for strategic decisions. **meta-ads-home-service** is a vertical-specific specialization of **ads-meta** tailored for home service businesses.
