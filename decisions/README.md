# Decisions

`decisions/` records policy and decision notes for conservative cleanup.

Current stage 3 decision files:

- `promotion-rules.md` - requirements before anything moves from `sources/` to
  `active/`.
- `websitepilot-policy.md` - open options for large WebsitePilot media.
- `generated-output-policy.md` - open policy for archived generated output.
- `sparse-sources-policy.md` - open policy for sparse source imports.

Decision notes should prefer `pending-user-decision` when evidence is not strong
enough for removal or promotion.
